====================
DIRECTION SENSE
====================

------------------------------------------------------------

BASIC IDEA
----------

Direction Sense questions test:

    • Understanding of directions
    • Turning logic
    • Distance calculation
    • Final position identification


------------------------------------------------------------

1) BASIC DIRECTIONS
-------------------

           North
             ↑
West   ←          →   East
             ↓
           South


------------------------------------------------------------

2) RIGHT & LEFT TURNS
---------------------

If facing North:

    Right → East
    Left  → West


If facing South:

    Right → West
    Left  → East


If facing East:

    Right → South
    Left  → North


If facing West:

    Right → North
    Left  → South


------------------------------------------------------------

3) CLOCKWISE MOVEMENT
---------------------

North → East → South → West → North


------------------------------------------------------------

4) ANTI-CLOCKWISE MOVEMENT
--------------------------

North → West → South → East → North


------------------------------------------------------------

5) DISTANCE CALCULATION
-----------------------

If movement forms straight lines:

Use Pythagoras Theorem

    Distance²

    = (Horizontal Distance)²
      +
      (Vertical Distance)²


Final Distance

    = √(Horizontal² + Vertical²)


------------------------------------------------------------

6) IMPORTANT KEYWORDS
---------------------

Towards

    = Facing that direction


Back

    = Turn 180°


Left turn

    = 90° anti-clockwise


Right turn

    = 90° clockwise


------------------------------------------------------------

7) SOLVING STRATEGY
-------------------

Step 1 → Draw rough diagram  
Step 2 → Track direction after each turn  
Step 3 → Mark final position  
Step 4 → Calculate shortest distance  


------------------------------------------------------------

8) COMMON TRAPS
---------------

• Forgetting to update direction after turn  
• Mixing clockwise and anti-clockwise  
• Ignoring 180° turn  
• Not drawing diagram
