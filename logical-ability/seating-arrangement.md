==========================
SEATING ARRANGEMENT
==========================

------------------------------------------------------------

BASIC IDEA
----------

Seating Arrangement questions test:

    • Logical positioning
    • Direction understanding
    • Relative placement
    • Arrangement accuracy


------------------------------------------------------------

1) TYPES OF SEATING ARRANGEMENT
--------------------------------

1) Linear Arrangement
       (People sitting in a straight line)

2) Circular Arrangement
       (People sitting around a table)

3) Square / Rectangular Arrangement
       (People sitting at corners or sides)


------------------------------------------------------------

2) LINEAR ARRANGEMENT RULES
----------------------------

If facing North:

    Left  → Your left
    Right → Your right


If facing South:

    Left  → Your right
    Right → Your left


Always check direction before solving.


------------------------------------------------------------

3) CIRCULAR ARRANGEMENT RULES
------------------------------

If all are facing centre:

    Right → Clockwise
    Left  → Anti-clockwise


If all are facing outside:

    Right → Anti-clockwise
    Left  → Clockwise


If mixed directions:

    Solve individually for each person.


------------------------------------------------------------

4) IMPORTANT TERMS
------------------

Immediate Left

    Person sitting exactly next on left


Second to the Right

    Skip one person, then next


Opposite (Circular)

    Total persons / 2


------------------------------------------------------------

5) SOLVING STRATEGY
-------------------

Step 1 → Draw diagram  
Step 2 → Fix definite positions first  
Step 3 → Place relative conditions  
Step 4 → Fill remaining positions  


------------------------------------------------------------

6) COMMON TRAPS
---------------

• Not checking facing direction  
• Mixing clockwise & anti-clockwise  
• Ignoring “immediate” keyword  
• Assuming position without confirmation
